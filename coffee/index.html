Our Story

More Than Coffee —
It's a Ritual

Born from a love of the craft, Velvet Bean is a space where exceptional coffee meets thoughtful hospitality. We source our beans directly from small-batch farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — then roast them fresh every morning right here in our open-view roastery.

🌍
Direct Trade Sourcing
We partner directly with farmers to ensure fair wages and the finest quality single-origin beans year-round.
🔥
In-House Roasting
Roasted fresh every morning in our open-view roastery for peak flavour and absolute freshness.
🌿
Sustainably Operated
100% compostable packaging, zero-waste kitchen, solar-powered, and carbon-offset delivery.
🏆
Award-Winning Coffee
Best Specialty Coffee in Andhra Pradesh, 2024 — South India Barista Guild.
Our Journey

Seven Years of
Crafting Perfection

From a tiny 8-seater kiosk to Vijayawada's most beloved coffee destination — our story is one of passion, patience, and really good espresso.

2018
The First Pour
Started with an 8-seat kiosk on MG Road, two espresso machines, and a dream. Served 30 cups on day one.
2020
The Roastery Opens
Installed our first drum roaster. Began sourcing directly from Ethiopian cooperatives.
2022
Expanded & Elevated
Moved to our current flagship location with rooftop seating, private pods, and a full food menu.
2024
Award & Recognition
Named Best Specialty Café in South India. Featured in Condé Nast Traveller India.
🏆
Best Specialty Coffee — South India 2024
Awarded by the South India Barista Guild, judged across 200+ cafés statewide on bean quality, barista craft, and guest experience.
🌱
Farm to Cup
Every bean traced to its origin farm. We visit our partner farms twice a year.
🎓
Barista School
All our baristas are certified Q-graders trained in-house over 6 months.
